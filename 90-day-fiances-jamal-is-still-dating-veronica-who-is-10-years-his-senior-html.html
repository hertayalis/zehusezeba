90 Day Fiances Jamal is still dating Veronica who is 10 years his senior

Fans of 90 Day Fiance were left stunned when Jamal, one of the show’s newest cast members, revealed he was in a relationship. Viewers are now curious to know if they are still together and intrigued by the age difference between Jamal and his girlfriend. Let’s delve into this love story and uncover the details.

Jamal’s Mystery Girlfriend:

Jamal, the son of Kim Menzies, made his debut on 90 Day Fiance not too long ago, causing quite a stir among fellow cast members. Since then, he’s been sharing affectionate photos with his special someone, piquing the interest of the show’s dedicated viewers. While some may not recognize her, longtime fans who followed Tim’s journey on 90 Day Fiance are already acquainted with Jamal’s new flame. But who is she, and what’s the age gap between them?

Meet Jamal’s Girlfriend:

Jamal is currently dating Veronica Rodriguez, who was previously engaged to Tim Malcolm. Veronica and Tim were in a relationship for a remarkable ten years before going their separate ways. Following their breakup, Veronica was with her partner, Grant Glaser, from 2019 until they decided to part ways.

Interestingly, Veronica first noticed Jamal on the Tell All segment of Kim’s season and reached out to him for recommendations when she planned a trip to San Diego. Their connection deepened as they spent time together during the vacation, including a memorable dinner. However, Jamal and Veronica have clarified that they are not in an exclusive relationship, and Jamal openly admitted to dating other people and being intimate with others. In contrast, Veronica has expressed that she is not currently seeing anyone else but remains open to new possibilities.

The Status of Veronica and Jamal’s Relationship:

As of the latest updates, Veronica and Jamal are still very much an item. In December, Jamal shared a video of them together, with his arm around Veronica, as they enjoyed a lighthearted moment, hand in hand. They were joined by other 90 Day stars, including Syngin.

Veronica also posted a photo from their trip to San Diego, and both of them follow each other on Instagram. Just a few weeks ago, Jamal playfully commented on their San Diego adventure, saying, “What happens in San Diego doesn’t stay in San Diego lmfao such a good night.”

Veronica responded with a playful acknowledgment, “Haha we DID get to keep it our little secret for a bit…” While it’s evident they are still spending time together, neither has officially announced exclusivity, as Jamal previously mentioned that he continues to date other people.

Age Is Just a Number:

Jamal, born in 1995, is currently 27 years old in 202In contrast, Veronica, as per IMDb, is 37 years old, making Jamal 10 years her junior. However, this age difference doesn’t appear to be a concern for either of them.

Veronica was 23 when she first met Tim, who was 28 at the time. During the 90 Day: The Single Life series, Veronica was 36 years old, showing that she’s no stranger to dating someone with a significant age gap. Their love story proves that age truly is just a number when two hearts connect.

Conclusion:

Despite the initial shock of Jamal’s reveal on 90 Day Fiance, his relationship with Veronica continues to flourish. The couple is navigating their journey with an age gap of 10 years, showing that love knows no bounds. As their story unfolds, fans eagerly anticipate further updates on this unique romance, proving that the quest for love on reality television can lead to unexpected and beautiful connections
