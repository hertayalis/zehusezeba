Parents, siblings, and early life

On April 1, 1993, in Newark, New Jersey, in the United States, singer Faith Evans gave birth to Chyna Tahjere Griffin, her eldest child. Her mother Faith lived with her biological father, Kiyamma Griffin, from 1992 to 1993.

Chyna moved in with her mother when they split up, as she had married Christopher Wallace, also known as “The Notorious B.I.G.” in August 1994.

In 1996, Chyna’s half-brother C.J. Wallace Jr. was born thanks to Faith and Biggie. Sadly, The Notorious B.I., her stepfather, passed away a year later.

Notorious was the subject of a 2009 biopic in which Wallace Jr. played a role she had previously played.

Her mother Evans wed Todd Russaw in 1998 following his passing. She gave birth to Ryder Evan Russaw and Joshua Russaw with Todd.

2011 saw Evans’ divorce from second husband Russaw. On July 17, 2018, Evans and her third husband, Stevie J., exchanged vows. Her spouse works as a record producer and DJ in America.

Chyna Griffin’s Career in Music and TV

Chyna began her musical career in the early 2010s. She released Chyna Doll-Grown Lady, her debut single, in January 2012.

A few years later, in 2016, Chyna teamed up with Kiyamma Griffin, her biological father, for the song “Alright.”

Griffin also made her TV debut in 2018 on Mahisa Dellinger’s program Mind Your Business.
