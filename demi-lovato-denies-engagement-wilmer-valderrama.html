Demi Lovato denies she is engaged to Wilmer Valderrama

 Posted on June 22, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  420 words  |  Larita Shotwell

    Demi Lovato may be head over heels for her boyfriend Wilmer Valderrama, but she isn't ready to get married just yet. The singer denied that she and Wilmer were engaged after she posted an Instagram photo that on Sunday that had fans jumping to conclusions.

    The snap showed Demi and Wilmer holding hands, with the 22-year-old wearing a gold band on her ring finger.

    View post on Instagram 

    Demi Lovato denied that she is engaged to Wilmer Valderrama

    "He's there for me when other's aren't… He supports me when others won't… He loves me when others don't," Demi captioned the photo, before editing to add: "(not engaged… yall are crazy)."

    The singer had hinted that she had an exciting announcement to make with another Instagram photo on Saturday, telling fans: "Exciting things coming veeeerrrryyyyy sooooon."

    Soon after she posted a photo of herself and Wilmer blowing a kiss for the camera, with the caption: "We have way too much fun together…"

    View post on Instagram 

    The couple have been dating for four years

    The series of loved-up photos prompted speculation from fans that Wilmer had popped the question to his girlfriend of four years. However while the former actor hasn't proposed just yet, it appears the couple could be ready to tie the knot in the not-too-distant future.

    Speaking to New Zealand radio show on Wednesday, Demi admitted she would love to marry Wilmer.

    "I am very, very in love with him," she said. "I think we'll probably wait a bit longer, but if he asked tomorrow, I'd say yes."

    View post on Instagram 

    Demi hinted that she had an exciting announcement to make

    Demi also revealed that their 12-year age gap initially caused comment and some concern when they first began dating.

    "I was 18 and he was 30 so people were definitely judgmental and even my parents were kind of, 'Why is someone his age wanting to date someone so young?" she explained. "And after a while everyone around us started to see what we have is real and incredible and very special, and we've been together ever since.

    "Obviously my family loves him [now]. People kind of stop doing that because you can see when we're together or even just from our Instagrams that we're very sweet to one another."
