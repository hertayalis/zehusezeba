Biography:

Bellamy Young was born on February 19, 1970 in Asheville, North Carolina. She studied at Yale University where she first majored in Physics. She eventually studied English and Theatre, graduating in 1991. It was during this time that she began her acting career on stage. In 1995, she made her screen debut in an episode of Another World. In the 2000s, she had roles in the films We Were Soldiers and Mission: Impossible III, among others.

Also during this time, she played recurring parts on the TV shows For the People, Scrubs, CSI: Miami and Dirty Sexy Money. In the 2010s, she played recurring character Beth Clemmons on Criminal Minds. She now stars as Melody “Mellie” Granton Scandal. This is a role she has been playing since 2012. Her most recent movie was in 2016’s The Night Stalker.
