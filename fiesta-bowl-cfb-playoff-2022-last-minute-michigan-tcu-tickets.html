There’s only one game separating the Michigan Wolverines and the TCU Horned Frogs from the 2023 NCAA College Football Playoff National Championship.

On Dec. 31, the two squads will face off against one another in this year’s Fiesta Bowl at Glendale, AZ’s State Farm Stadium, which determines whose season ends in 2022 or 2023.

Jim Harbaugh’s No. 2 Wolverines come into the contest following a perfect 13-0 season, thanks largely to the standout play of quarterback J.J. McCarthy and wide receiver Ronnie Bell.

Meanwhile, Sonny Dykes’ third-ranked Horned Frogs enter the semifinal sporting a 12-1 record.

Now, with everything on the line, you can still purchase last-minute tickets to catch the contenders in the highly-anticipated matchup.

At the time of publication, ticket prices start at $160 before fees on Vivid Seats.

Lower-level seats can be found for as low as $294 before fees.

All prices are subject to fluctuation.

Kickoff is at 2 p.m. PST.
