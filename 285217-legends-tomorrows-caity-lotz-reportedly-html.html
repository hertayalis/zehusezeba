'Legends of Tomorrow's' Caity Lotz Reportedly Had Affair with Ruby Rose Who Later Sparked Dating Rum

 Posted on July 5, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  420 words  |  Larita Shotwell

Fans mainly recognize Caity Lotz as Star City vigilante Sarah Lance on "The Legends of Tomorrow." The actress has also starred in other films such as "The Pact" and "Death Valley."

In 2020, Caity Lotz began sparking rumors that she was dating "Pitch Perfect" star Ruby Rose after photos of the two emerged in December 2020.

They began posting photos of each other on their Instagram accounts, raising speculations of a possible romance. After the first photos came out, Lotz then shared another photo of them at Potato Chip Rock in Ramona, California.

Left: Caity Lotz with Ruby Rose: Photo: Instagram.com/caitylotz Right: Ruby Rose with Rob Raco | Photo: Instagram.com/rubyrose

Towards the end of the year, the couple celebrated Lotz's birthday together, with Ruby declaring that Lotz was one of the most precious people she knew.

The seemingly happy couple celebrated Lotz's 34-year mark by sharing beautiful photos. In one, the "Batwoman" actress rested her head on Lotz's shoulder as two stared serenely at the camera with a lit-up pink sky behind them.

Rose captioned the photo, sharing that it was her favorite human's birthday. Even though the couple did not confirm their relationship, more images posted in late May and early July seemed to calm the rumors.

However, an alleged split saw Lotz return to her role as White Canary on the CW's Arrowverse television series. Photos showed the actress on the show's set in Vancouver.

She was in the company of her costars, Lisseth Chavez and Olivia Swan, and looked magical in a red-tailed coat worn over a white dress shirt and a brown vest.

"The Machine" actress wore a black hat, fishnet stockings, black shorts, and a matching bow tie to match the look. She completed the ensemble with black boots.

Besides playing the role of a League of Assassins member suffering from rage issues after being resurrected by the mystical Lazarus Pit, Lotz also directed the 100th episode of the show.

And while Lotz kept herself busy at work, more rumors sparked that Rose was dating Rob Raco. "The Meg" actress shared cryptic photos of the couple as they got cozy on a balcony.

In one photo, the "Riverdale" actor plants a kiss on Rose's cheek, while in a second, the two hugged passionately. They then tagged each other in their suggestive Instagram post.

The photos left their fans confused about whether they were dating, seeing as Rose is openly gay and gender-fluid. However, the two did not confirm whether they were indeed a couple.
