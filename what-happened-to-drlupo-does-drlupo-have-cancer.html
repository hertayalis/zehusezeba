Is Dr. Lupo a Cancer Patient?

No, Dr. Lupo is not a cancerous person. Dr. Lupo’s extraordinary efforts to improve the health and well-being of cancer patients are a testament to his commitment to charity.

The most noteworthy charitable endeavor of Dr. Lupo is his long-standing collaboration with St. Jude Children’s Research Hospital.
